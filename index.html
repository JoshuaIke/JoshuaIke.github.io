Fun Facts

  • Connect 4 was invented by Milton Bradley in Febuary 1974
  • There are 4,531,985,219,092 possible positions on a traditional Connect 4 board
  • The average Connect 4 game takes 1 to 10 minutes to play
  • There are annual Connect 4 game tournaments held across the globe
  • Legend says that Captain Cook was one of the first to play Connect 4
  • My CSP teacher absolutely destroyed me once in a game of Connect 4

How to play

  1. Decide who plays first. Players will alternate turns after playing a checker
  2. The player starting the first game will play second in the next game
  3. On your turn, drop one of your checks down Any of the slots in the top grid.
  4. Play alternates until one player gets Four checkers of their color in a row.
  5. The four in a row can be horizontal, vertical or diagonal
